Our Story

About Us

Nutrigrain Labs is a food manufacturing and private label partner specializing in grains, pulses, millets, spices, and nuts. We work with startups and established brands to deliver consistent, scalable food products.

Based in Bhilwara, Rajasthan, our state-of-the-art facility combines traditional sourcing expertise with modern food processing technology — ensuring every product meets the highest standards of quality and safety.
